VISENDUS 2022

By SEDS in University of Colombo

A chapter under SEDS Sri Lanka


About Us

VISENDUS 2022: A visit to the Sri Lanka Planetarium will be the first-ever physical project organized by SEDS UOC since its inauguration in 2019. Even though the country’s first and only planetarium is located inside the University of Colombo premises itself, many undergraduates are not aware of the specialty of this national monument. SEDS UOC is planning to give them the opportunity to visit and explore the importance of the Sri Lanka Planetarium. This visit will be followed by some exciting fun activities at the University of Colombo Grounds. SEDS UOC is willing to provide support in projects organized by the planetarium and plans to organize many projects in the future with the cooperation of the Sri Lanka Planetarium.

SEDS UOC (Students for the Exploration and Development of Space – University of Colombo) is a society of the University of Colombo that consists of undergraduates who are enthusiastic about various aspects related to space exploration and development.

Being the national SEDS group, SEDS Sri Lanka governs 16 chapters established under it with members from a number of government and private universities, as well as a separate chapter for school children named SEDS Juniors.
